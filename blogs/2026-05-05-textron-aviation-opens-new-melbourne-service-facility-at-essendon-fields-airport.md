(NYSE: TXT) company, today announced its new service facility at Essendon Fields Airport in Melbourne is now open for customers, expanding factory-direct support for Cessna, Beechcraft and Hawker customers across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region. The purpose-built facility strengthens Textron Aviation&rsquo;s global service network and reflects the company&rsquo;s long-term commitment to expanding capacity and enhancing customer support throughout aircraft ownership.</p> <p id="news-body-cta">This press release features multimedia. View the full release here: <a href="https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260505135152/en/" rel="nofollow">https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260505135152/en/</a></p> <div id="bwbodyimg" style="width: 480px; float: left; padding: 0px 20px 0px 0px;"><img src="https://mms.businesswire.com/media/20260505135152/en/2792221/4/Essendon_Hangar_%28High_Res%29-489-edit.jpg" alt="Textron Aviation opens new Melbourne service facility at Essendon Fields airport, expanding support for Cessna, Beechcraft and Hawker customers in APAC"> <p style="font-size: 85%;">Textron Aviation opens new Melbourne service facility at Essendon Fields airport, expanding support for Cessna, Beechcraft and Hawker customers in APAC</p> </div> <p>&ldquo;We&rsquo;ve supported customers in Australia for decades, and we continue to invest where our customers tell us they need more capacity and faster access to factory direct expertise,&rdquo; said Brian Rohloff, senior vice president, Global Customer Support, Textron Aviation. &ldquo;The Essendon Fields facility is a significant investment in a highly important region, strengthening our service network and expanding service capability, parts access and technical support across Australia and the Asia Pacific region.&rdquo;</p> <p>The new facility more than doubles the footprint of Textron Aviation&rsquo;s previous Essendon Fields operation to more than 35,000 square feet (3,343 square meters) and is designed to support the more than 1,400 Cessna, Beechcraft and Hawker aircraft operating across the Asia-Pacific region. Developed based on customer feedback, the location features expanded space for servicing aircraft to help reduce downtime, an on&#8209;site Textron Aviation parts stockroom to improve parts availability and a more comfortable customer lounge for use while aircraft are being serviced.</p> <p>The Essendon Fields service center complements Textron Aviation&rsquo;s broader investment strategy to strengthen regional support across Australia, alongside recent facility expansions and upgrades in <a href="https://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fmedia.txtav.com%2F238342-textron-aviation-expands-presence-in-australia-to-modernize-facilities-and-increase-parts-in-region-announces-name-change-of-premiair-%2F&amp;esheet=54527269&amp;newsitemid=20260505135152&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Perth&amp;index=3&amp;md5=19cdd75ea4224e31f860c31fc92ae87b" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">Perth</a>. Located at one of Australia&rsquo;s most established aviation hubs, the new facility also reflects close collaboration with Essendon Fields Airport to support the continued growth of business aviation and aircraft maintenance in the region.</p> <p>&ldquo;Our investment in the new Textron Aviation service center underscores Essendon Fields&rsquo; commitment to building Australia&rsquo;s most capable and connected business aviation precinct,&rdquo; said Brandan Pihan, CEO, Essendon Fields. &ldquo;As the closest airport to the Melbourne CBD, Essendon Fields is the most convenient choice for business aviation customers. Textron Aviation&rsquo;s long-term commitment ensures the retention of skilled jobs and service capability at Essendon. It also advances our Airport Master Plan by consolidating operations on the main airfield, improving safety and efficiency, and responds to strong demand for new hangar space.&rdquo;</p> <p>Textron Aviation will host a formal grand opening event for the Essendon Fields service facility in August, inviting members of the media, customers and community leaders to officially mark the opening of the site. Additional details will be shared closer to the celebration.</p> <p><strong>About Textron Aviation Customer Support <br></strong>Textron Aviation, through its Beechcraft and Cessna brands, is renowned for its unrivaled global service network dedicated to complete life-cycle support.
