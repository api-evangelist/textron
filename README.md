# Textron

Textron Inc. is a $14.8 billion Fortune 500 multi-industry company employing 34,000 people worldwide. Textron operates through five business segments: Bell (military and commercial helicopters), Textron Aviation (Cessna, Beechcraft, Hawker aircraft), Industrial (Kautex, E-Z-GO, Jacobsen), Textron Systems (defense and autonomous systems), and Textron Financial.

- **[Bell](https://www.bellflight.com/)** — Military rotorcraft (V-22 Osprey, AH-1Z, UH-1Y) and commercial helicopters (Bell 429, 505, 525)
- **[Textron Aviation](https://txtav.com/)** — Cessna (Citation jets, Caravan) and Beechcraft (King Air, Bonanza) aircraft

- **[Kautex](https://www.textron.com/content/kautex)** — Automotive plastic fuel systems and structural components
- **Textron Specialized Vehicles** — E-Z-GO golf carts, Jacobsen grounds maintenance equipment
